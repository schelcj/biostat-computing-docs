# Accounts and Allocations #

### Accounts ###

Cluster accounts are available to all biostat faculty, students and researchers.
There is currently no cost associated with an account allocation but that may
change in the future. Faculty may request an account directly by emailing
[sph-biostat-help@umich.edu][]. Students will have to have their advisor
request the account for them. Researchers have your your PI request your account.

### Login Credentials ###

Your login username will be your umich [uniqname][] and your password will be your
UMICH [kerberos][] password.

### Access Details ###

All access to the cluster is over ssh within the Umich network via the [login nodes][]. 

__HOSTNAME:__
: *biostat-login.sph.umich.edu*

__USERNAME:__
: [uniqname][]

__PASSWORD:__
: Your Umich [kerberos][] password
